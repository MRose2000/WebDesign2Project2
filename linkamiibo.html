Link TM (Archer)

Series: The Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild Release Date: March 03, 2017 Presented by: Nintendo Link TM is the main character in The Legend of Zelda TM games. A young boy living in Hyrule, Link is often given the task of rescuing Princess Zelda TM and Hyrule from the Gerudo thief Ganondorf. Humble to the end, Link is known not merely as a hero but as a symbol of courage, strength and wisdom as well.
